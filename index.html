About Us

We are a non-profit organization from New York who loves watching movies and bringing people together. Our idea was to combine things we enjoy and make something for everybody. Our mission is simple: Celebrate art of cinematography. No mather is it a big holywood blockbuster or smaller independant movie. We want you to be part of our mission and help us grow. This is our first year of festival and we hope to have many more. Support us on our social media and we hope to see you soon.
